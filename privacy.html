Privacy Policy & Data Protection

Your data security is our top priority. We operate in compliance with GDPR and Turkish KVKK regulations.

Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ticevo ("we", "our", or "the Company") collects, uses, stores and protects your personal data when you use our services.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following personal data when you use our services:

  • Identity Information: Name, surname, email address, phone number
  • Company Information: Company name, tax identification number, address
  • Marketplace Account Information: API keys, store IDs, seller information
  • Transaction Data: Product information, order data, stock movements, pricing data
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, session information, cookie data
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment history (credit card details are processed by the payment provider)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and managing marketplace integration services
  • Performing stock, price and order synchronization operations
  • Account management and customer support
  • Invoice generation and payment processing
  • Service quality improvement and analytics
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Implementing security measures

3. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted with TLS/SSL
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 + SHA256 algorithm (100,000 iterations)
  • Access Control: Role-based authorization and multi-tenant data isolation
  • Infrastructure Security: Firewall, DDoS protection and regular security updates
  • Backup: Regular data backups and disaster recovery plan

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Marketplace Platforms: Due to the nature of our service, your product and order data is shared with the marketplaces you connect (Trendyol, Hepsiburada, N11, Amazon, Etsy, OpenCart)
  • Payment Providers: Your payment transactions are processed through secure payment infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be shared pursuant to court orders or legal regulations

5. Amazon Services API Data Protection

For data accessed through Amazon Selling Partner API, we provide additional safeguards:

  • Amazon API data is processed solely for synchronization purposes
  • Data is stored in compliance with Amazon's Data Protection Policy requirements
  • API access keys are stored in encrypted form
  • Data is used only for operations authorized by the customer
  • When a customer closes their account, all Amazon API data is deleted within 30 days
  • We do not share Amazon Marketplace data with any unauthorized third parties
  • Access to Amazon data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We maintain audit logs for all access to Amazon Selling Partner API data
  • Amazon data is not used for any purpose other than providing the authorized services to the customer

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK), you have the following rights:

  • Right to know whether your personal data is being processed
  • Right to request information about the processing of your personal data
  • Right to know the purpose of processing and whether data is used in accordance with that purpose
  • Right to know the third parties to whom your personal data has been transferred
  • Right to request correction of incomplete or inaccurate personal data
  • Right to request deletion or destruction of your personal data
  • Right to object to automated processing that produces results against you
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ticevo.com.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: For the duration the account is active + 30 days after account closure
  • Transaction logs: 1 year
  • Billing data: 10 years (legal requirement)
  • Marketplace API data: For the duration the integration is active

8. Cookie Policy

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for session management and security
  • Performance Cookies: Anonymous analytics cookies to measure site performance

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored on servers located in Turkey and the European Union. If data transfer to other countries is necessary, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR requirements.

10. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy:

  • Email: privacy@ticevo.com
  • Web: ticevo.com

11. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. In case of significant changes, you will be notified via your registered email address.
